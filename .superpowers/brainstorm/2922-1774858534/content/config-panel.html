B. All Fields Visible (Scrollable)

All parameters visible in a single scrollable panel. Pill nav at top for quick-jump to sections. No hiding, no expanding — everything is always editable. Dense but transparent.

Recommendation: A (Accordion) — keeps the panel compact and focused. The one-line summaries give instant overview of the full config. Expanding one section at a time reduces cognitive load.
